Grid Bots provide a configurable automated trading strategy that places a series of buy and sell orders across a predefined price range, enabling continuous “buy-low / sell-high” execution within volatile or sideways markets. This bot algorithm automatically executes trades according to grid parameters rather than price direction, aiming to capture market oscillations and generate incremental profit through repeated cycles.

Grid Bots are fully programmable via API, allowing developers to create, configure, activate, monitor, and manage grid-based trading automation across supported exchanges and connectors.
